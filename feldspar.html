Feldspar

Feldspar is a key raw material used in ceramic, glass, enamel, welding electrodes and paint industries. It acts as a fluxing agent and helps reduce melting temperature while improving product strength and durability.

Chemical Composition

Component Percentage (%)
SiO₂ 65 – 69%
Al₂O₃ 16 – 18%
K₂O 9 – 12%
Na₂O 2 – 4%
Fe₂O₃ 0.1 – 0.4%
